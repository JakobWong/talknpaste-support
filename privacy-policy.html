How TalknPaste Works

TalknPaste uses advanced AI models that run entirely on your Mac to convert speech to text. Here's what happens when you use the app:

  1. You click to start recording
  2. Your Mac's microphone captures audio
  3. The AI model processes the audio locally
  4. Text is generated and copied to your clipboard
  5. The audio data is immediately discarded

At no point in this process is any data stored, transmitted, or shared.
